# ClipboardBar
macOS Clipboard Bar、Menu Bar Apps
## Highlights
- You can view the macOS copy history again.
- You can choose how many copies of the copy history to save.
- You can also delete the copy history.
## Installation
1) Download the latest DMG
2) Open the DMG and drag ClipboardBar into Applications.
3) Launch ClipboardBar and grant the requested permissions.
## Requirements
- macOS 14.0 or later (It might work in other versions as well.)
- MacBooks with Apple Silicon and Intel series only
- Permissions as needed: Gatekeeper, Other permissions (It might still work without it.)
